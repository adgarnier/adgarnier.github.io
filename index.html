About Me

I'm a biochemist and computational biologist based in Montreal, Canada, with a passion for solving complex problems at the intersection of biology and data. I hold an MSc in Biochemistry from McGill University, where I investigated protein vulnerabilities in cancer through a blend of computational screening and experimental validation.

Currently, I'm an Associate Scientist at IQVIA Laboratories, where I'm automating data workflows, optimizing LIMS infrastructure, and conducting ELISA-based assays for both R&D and clinical projects.
